Positive Thoughts for Lupus Patients
Positive Thoughts
(Favorites of Cyndee Miller)

Love yourself.

Ask and ye shall receive.

Hakuna Matata (no worries, from "The Lion King")

Don't worry, be happy! (Bobby McFerrin)

Enjoy the moment.

For every minute you are angry, you lose 60 seconds of happiness.

Accentuate the positive, not the negative.

It takes both the sun and the rain to make a rainbow.

Success is getting up one more time than you fall down.

When you get to the end of your rope, tie a knot and hang on! (Franklin Delano Roosevelt)

"The Daily Motivator" by Ralph S. Marston, Jr.
