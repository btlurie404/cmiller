Lupus Doctor Information

  • AIM DocFinder
    Contains the licensing background and disciplinary information of physicians and other health care practitioners.

  • Arizona Medical Board
    Under "Doctor Search", contains education and training, board investigations and actions, and malpractice/criminal information for physicians or physician assistants.

  • The American Medical Association
    DoctorFinder under "For Patients" section - search for a licensed doctor by name, specialty or location. Also, provides information about the doctor's medical school, residency, internships, and specialty certifications.
