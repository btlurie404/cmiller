How To Determine If A Health Site Is Reliable How To Determine If A Health Site Is Reliable

  • It has real doctors behind it.
      Check the site's Advisory Board link for physicians with university or medical center affiliations.
  • It may have "HON CODE" seal.
      Sites that are accredited by the Health on the Net Foundation are more likely to uphold ethical standards of medical reporting.
  • It is unbiased.
      Check the About Us link to understand who's behind the information.
  • It is current.
      Check the Last Updated link to see when the information has been updated.
